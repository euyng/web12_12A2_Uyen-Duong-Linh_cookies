Recipe

Raspberries Jam Cookies

Hi~! Our group has three members: Thuy Duong, Tu Uyen, and Bao Linh. We all share a passion for baking and experimenting with new recipes. The dessert our group decided to make is Chocolate Chip Cookie with Raspberry Jam. Our recipe includes about a hundred and fifty grams of brown sugar, fifty grams of white sugar, two hundred grams of melted butter, two hundred and fifty grams of flour, three quarters of a teaspoon of baking soda, three hundred and fifty grams of chocolate chips, and two to three tablespoons of raspberry jam. Although the ingredients are quite familiar, when combined they create a flavor that is both comforting and unique. We hope this dessert will inspire you to try baking with us!

Luong Thuy Duong
Nguyen Tu Uyen
Ng. Le Bao Linh
